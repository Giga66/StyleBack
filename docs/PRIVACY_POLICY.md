# StyleBack Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: May 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how StyleBack ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you install or use the StyleBack app in connection with your Shopify-supported store.

## Information We Collect

When you install the App, we are automatically able to access certain types of information from your Shopify account:
- **Store Information:** Store name, domain, email, and timezone.
- **Customer Information:** We access customer names and email addresses strictly for the purpose of sending automated cart recovery emails.
- **Checkout Information:** We access abandoned checkout data (cart contents, product IDs, quantities) to generate personalized cart recovery emails.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and operate the StyleBack service, specifically to:
- Detect abandoned checkouts on your storefront.
- Queue and send personalized recovery emails (Reservation, Pairings, and Offer emails) to the abandoned checkout customers on your behalf.
- Analyze recovery rates and display performance statistics on your StyleBack dashboard.

We do NOT use your customers' information to market our own services, nor do we sell, rent, or trade your customers' data to third parties.

## Data Retention

We retain abandoned checkout data and email schedules only for as long as necessary to complete the cart recovery sequence. Once a cart is recovered or the final email is sent, the raw checkout data is purged from our active processing queues. 

## Changes
We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

## Contact Us
For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at support@styleback.app or via the Live Chat widget in your StyleBack dashboard.
